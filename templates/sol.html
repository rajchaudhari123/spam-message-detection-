How We Keep Your Messages Safe

At Spam Detection, we take your privacy and security seriously. Here's how we ensure your messages are safe from spam:

  • report: A good way to stop spam texts is to report them and cut the spammers off at the source. There are four ways to report spam texts: forward the spam message to 7726, report the spam directly in your messaging app, report it to your wireless provider, or report spam texts to the FTC (in the US)..
  • do not interact with the message: First, if you receive a spam text, do not interact with the message. If you click a link or attachment in a spam message, it could trigger malware that infects your phone. You also don't want to respond.
